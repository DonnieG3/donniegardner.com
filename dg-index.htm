About Me

20 years of driving growth in B2B and B2C classified marketplaces

Hello! I'm Donnie Gardner, a Senior Product Manager with two decades of experience transforming digital marketplaces and driving user engagement. My journey began in graphic design, which gave me a unique perspective on user experience that I've carried throughout my product management career.

Currently at Trader Interactive, I lead a $20M Private Party platform serving over 100,000 users. I'm passionate about using data-driven insights to create products that not only meet business objectives but genuinely improve users' lives. My approach combines strategic thinking with hands-on execution, always focusing on measurable outcomes.

What drives me is the intersection of technology, user experience, and business growth. I believe the best products are built when cross-functional teams collaborate effectively, iterate quickly, and stay focused on solving real problems for real people.
